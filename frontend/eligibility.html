👣 Next Steps

    🎂

    Minimum Age: 18

    You must be at least 18 years old on the qualifying date (1st January of the year) to register as a voter.

    🇮🇳

    Indian Citizenship

    Only Indian citizens can vote in Indian elections. NRIs with valid Indian passports may register under special provisions.

    📋

    Electoral Roll

    Even if eligible, your name must appear on the Electoral Roll. Register using Form 6 at voters.eci.gov.in.

    🚫

    Disqualifications

    You may be disqualified if declared of unsound mind by a court, or convicted of certain offences. Check ECI guidelines.
